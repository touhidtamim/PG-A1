Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation drives a country forward by fostering creativity, boosting efficiency, and enabling technological and economic advancements.

Sustainability

Sustainability propels a country forward by preserving resources, promoting long-term development, and ensuring a balanced environment for future generations.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure advances a country by supporting economic growth, improving connectivity, and enhancing the quality of life for its people.

Empowerment

Empowerment drives a country forward by enabling individuals to contribute, innovate, and create positive social and economic change.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Bangladesh 2.0 envisions a nation built on justice, human rights, and freedom of speech. It emphasizes learning from past mistakes to honor sacrifices made during transformative times. The youth are seen as pivotal in driving sustainable development and inclusive growth, leading the country toward an equitable society. The vision promotes an economy where technological advancements and progress benefit all, eliminating disparities. A world of 'three zeros' is central to this future: zero net carbon emissions, zero wealth concentration, and zero unemployment, achieved by fostering entrepreneurship and innovation."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh

Date: 28-10-2023

Dhaka Launches First-Ever Metro Rail Service

Date: 20-12-2022

Time: 06.42 PM

Bangladesh has launched its first-ever metro rail service in Dhaka. The 11.73-kilometer stretch is expected to ease traffic congestion, improve public transportation, and enhance the city’s infrastructure. This project is a major step towards modernizing Dhaka's transit system.

Date: 11-05-2020

Time: 04.17 AM

Bangladesh has launched its first satellite, Bangabandhu-1. The satellite will enhance communication services, reduce dependency on foreign satellites, and boost the nation’s space capabilities. This historic launch marks a significant step in Bangladesh’s technological progress.
